A note from Lena

You've done the kind of work that most people only think about doing. The fact that you're here — enrolled, committed, reading this — means something. Not as flattery. As observation.

The Presence Collective will ask things of you that the initial experience didn't. The protocols are more demanding. The community will hold you accountable in ways you may not expect. There will be a week — probably week six — where it gets harder before it gets clearer.

That's the week the practice becomes yours. Not a technique you're trying. Not a framework you're learning. A thing you actually do, because you've proven to yourself that you can sustain it.

I'll see you on November 4. Come as you are.
